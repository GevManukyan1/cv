About me

Hello, I am Gevorg Manukyan, born 09.05.2000, in Verin Artashat village, Ararat Region, Armenia.

From 2006 to 2018 I studied at the local school and then went to National Polytechnic University of Armenia (NPUA) and now I am a third year student in Radio Communication.

From 2018 to 2020 I served in the Army, in the third protective service in Artsakh, after which I came back and continued my education.

From May 2021 I started learning programming in the IT-G company, after which I continued my lessons on my own and passed the entire course in 7-8 months, after which I engaged in self-development and in May 2022 I was an intern in IT Artashat company and in June of the same year I got a job offer and started to work there until now.

During my school years I participated in different olympiads (Maths, Physics, Geography), and at the university I was the sports director of the ASUE Institute, participated in and also organized a lot of events.

From 2008-2016 I played professional football and 5 years in a row I participated in the championship of Armenia, and now I play amateur football.

From 2016 to 2018 I was engaged in Street Workout and for 2 years in a row I participated in the championship of Armenia, and after quitting it I keep on going to the gym. My hobbies are: games, films, sport, spending time with my friends or family.

I'm a junior frontEnd developer. After getting acquainted with programming, I realized that I really like it and I like doing it, I realized that this is the profession that I want to have for the rest of my life.
